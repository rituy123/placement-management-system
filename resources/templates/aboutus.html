Placement and Training Department

Our Institute is located near Tumkur, a well known place of learning. Our college is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belgaum (Karnataka), recognized by AICTE, New Delhi, & by Government of Karnataka. Within a short span of 10 years we have made our presence felt strongly in this competitive educational sector and we have many firsts to our credit. Our ISO Certification and NBA Accreditation speak of our commitment to quality systems and education. Our credentials are well appreciated by many industries, engineering institutes, professional bodies, government organizations etc. In the recent past our institute has been rated A Grade and is included among “THE BEST ENGINEERING COLLEGES IN INDIA” by Career360, a popular magazine promoted by OUTLOOK Group.
